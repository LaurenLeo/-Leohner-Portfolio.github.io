About Lauren

Front-end developer with an aspiration to merge expansive background in art and design with technology.

I recently graduated from Grand Circus, an intensive 10-week coding bootcamp, where I learned the web development skills necessary to devote my passion for art/design to the thriving tech community in Detroit.

My creative career began at Southern Illinois University, where I earned a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Studio Art with emphasis in Oil Painting and a minor in Art History.

After college, I transitioned to making and selling jewelry online. Unsatisfied with the e-commerce sites out there I became interested in web development as a means of accurately representing my creative work.

I quickly grew passionate about Front-end web development and see coding as another medium for me to flex my creative problem solving skills.

Hypenings

Hypenings; Final Group Project from Grand Circus. An app that lets users post events at their current location. Other users find events within a one mile radius. Events are displayed as markers on a map and as flyers on the page. Users can customize flyers by choosing an icon image and background color. We used AngularJS and SQL as well as the Google Maps API and Mailjet API.
